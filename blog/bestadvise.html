Some of the best advise I've recieved

Here are some good advises I've got that I want to pass on to others.

"Dabble more in text based operating systems" - 2018

This was told to me by a senior while when I first tried using the Raspberry Pi. I was using Windows as my daily driver then. I didn't understand why anyone would want to use a terminal over a GUI back then, but I realised the importance of this after I started using Linux as my main operating system.

"Attend lots of hackathons and build networks. Will do wonders in your career trust me." - 2019

Self explanatory. The amount of opportunities you will get scales with the size of your network.
